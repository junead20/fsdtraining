About Skill Academy

Bridging the gap between traditional academic education and high-demand industry skills since 2015.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in technology education, creating a seamless pathway for aspiring individuals to gain practical skills, secure rewarding tech careers, and build innovative solutions for tomorrow's challenges.

Our Mission

To deliver high-impact, hands-on technical bootcamps led by industry veterans. We focus on project-based learning, comprehensive career mentorship, and curriculum that adapts dynamically to modern technological trends.

Our Story

Institute History & Milestones

A look at how we grew from a small computer lab into a premium training ecosystem with thousands of alumni.

2015

The Beginning

Skill Academy was founded in Hyderabad with a single computer classroom, teaching Core Java and Web Essentials to 25 passionate local students.

2018

Expanding Curriculums

Introduced Python Programming, Cloud Architecture, and Data Science cohorts. Expanded campus to accommodate over 200 concurrent trainees.

2021

Going Online & Placements

Launched a specialized online LMS portal. Formed official placement partnerships with 50+ tech firms to enable direct hire pipelines for our graduates.

2026

AI Era & Leadership

Upgraded course catalogs with advanced Generative AI and Machine Learning bootcamps, training over 5,000 global students annually.
