Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers!

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our portal and academic services.

To register on the portal, click the "Register Now" button on the home page. Fill in your personal details including name, roll number, email, mobile number, and other required information. After submission, you'll receive a confirmation email with your login credentials.

You can view your attendance records by logging into your account and navigating to the Dashboard section. Your attendance percentage is displayed along with a detailed breakdown of present and absent days.

You can view your digital ID card in the ID Card section of the portal. To download, right-click on the ID card and select "Save image as" or use the print function to save it as a PDF.

Exam results are typically published within 2-3 weeks after the exam date. You'll receive an email notification when your results are available. Visit the Dashboard or Results section to view your detailed score breakdown.

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a password reset link. Click the link to create a new password.

Go to your Profile section, click the "Edit Profile" button, update your information, and save the changes. You can modify your contact details, address, and profile picture.
